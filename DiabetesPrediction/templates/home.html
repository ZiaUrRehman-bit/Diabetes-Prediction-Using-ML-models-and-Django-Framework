Main goals of PREDICTOR

The mains goals of DECIDER are to develop diagnostic tools and treatments for high-grade serous ovarian cancer with the help of AI methods. The aim is to identify earlier those patients who do not respond well to the first-line treatments, and to find effective treatments to patients with a drug-resistant cancer.

We also study the legal issues that impede or slow down the use of new treatments in order to facilitate commercialization and availability of personalized therapies in an ethically and legally sustainable manner.
